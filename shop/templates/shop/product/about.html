About USTAWI

USTAWI is an African Online store, that provides an opportunity for our local artisans, fashion project owners and designers to showcase and sell their unique african designs ranging from furniture (home decor, office decor), jewellery and so on. Offering customers Africa's diverse Fashion taste under one roof

HOW IT WORKS

We welcome multiple merchants to add their unique line of products to our repository, which then get displayed on our site, providing a single store where there is a union of African Fashion Products.
